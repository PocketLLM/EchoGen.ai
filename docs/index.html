Detailed Features

Explore the powerful features in detail

Convert any URL or text into podcast format with AI-powered content adaptation. The system intelligently structures the content into a conversational format.

Create natural-sounding conversations between multiple AI speakers. Choose from a variety of voices and assign speakers for each part of the content.

Adjust the tone, speed, and emotion of each speaker to match the context of the conversation. Fine-tune the delivery for a natural listening experience.

Export your podcast in MP3 format and share it directly to your favorite platforms or save it locally on your device.
